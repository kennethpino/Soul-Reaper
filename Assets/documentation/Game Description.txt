Soul Ripper

The players is a death ripper who is assigned to a police station after a bomb attack.
There are a total of 10 victims in the building and his job is to find them and tip their soul and send them to heaven or hell.
Some of the victims is clearly visible where they should be sent though the player is free to send them wherever he likes, and some other victims is not clear what fate they deserve and some digging needs to be done beefore deciding.
The game ends once all the souls have been sent away and the player is given a score.
